ABOUT US

Who Are We?

We are an artisinal home goods shop specializing in pillows of all sorts. Located in Pittburgh, we opened shop in early 2020 with the hopes of bringing sustainable yet affordable pillows for everyone.

Where Do We Produce?

We know that this is an important question for anyone looking to shop sustainably and we would like to be as transparent as possible. All out products are designed, developed and produced locally in Pittsburgh. Our factories are all Bluesign and Oeko-Tex certified. What does that mean? That means that we make sure that the fabrics we are working with are 100% natural.
